FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is NiftyQuant SEBI-registered?
Yes. We're registered as a Research Analyst (RA) with SEBI — registration number INH000012345. We provide research and signals; we do not manage capital on your behalf. Execution always happens in your own Kite account.
Do I need a Zerodha Kite account?
For one-click routing, yes — Kite Connect is the only execution venue we integrate with today. You can still use Starter without Kite — you'll just need to place orders manually in your own broker.
What's the minimum capital to start?
There's no hard floor, but we recommend ₹2,00,000 minimum. Position sizes scale with your equity, and small accounts have trouble respecting stops on higher-priced names like NESTLEIND or BAJFINANCE.
How is this different from a Telegram tip channel?
Three differences. (1) Every signal has explicit entry, stop, and target — no "buy at CMP". (2) Every signal is walk-forward backtested before it ships — no "this stock looks good". (3) Every trade is logged with timestamps, fills, and P&L — no screenshots, no selective memory.
Can I cancel?
Anytime, no questions asked. Cancel before the next renewal date and you won't be charged again. Existing track-record and journal data stays accessible read-only.
What happens on a flat or bearish day?
The dashboard says so plainly — "No fresh signals today — next scan at 16:15 IST." The system is designed to underfit; on average, ~3 of 5 weekdays produce zero A-grade signals. Patience is part of the edge.
